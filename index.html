THE ARENA

75th Annual Pixel Games
You are Tribute #12. Twenty-three others from all twelve districts have been dropped into this arena with you. Grab a weapon from the Cornucopia, survive the bloodbath, and be the last one standing. Patches of tall grass across the arena will hide you from a distance — use them. The safe zone will shrink — the gas outside it does not forgive. Watch for the Career pack — tributes from Districts 1, 2 and 4 hunt together and gang up on a single target, though they'll turn on each other once the field thins out. By Day 3 the Gamemakers release mutts into the arena — they hunt everyone alike. Hunger and energy drain over time — forage berries near bushes to stay fed, but some are poisonous and will stun and sicken you.
